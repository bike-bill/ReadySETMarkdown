An open source library for editing diagrams (boxes and arrows). ##### ::GPA ::*n.* Grade Point Average. GPA is a float between 0.00 and 4.00, accurate to 2 decimal places. Computed from average of completed course grades in transcript weighted by course units. Used to determine student ranking, and to trigger Dean's List and academic probation. #### I &nbsp; ##### ::ICS ::*n.* Acronym for the [School of Information and Computer Science](http://www.ics.uci.edu/) at [UC Irvine](http://www.uci.edu/). #### S &nbsp; ##### ::Student ::*n.* A person who attends a school to earn a degree. Persistent attributes include: student\_id\_number (primary key), GPA, major, and years\_at\_school. ##### ::Senior ::*n.* A senior is special type of [undergraduate](#undergraduate) who has a certain number of course credits on his or her transcript. Years\_at\_school does not determine senior standing.
